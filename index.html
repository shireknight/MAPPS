A MAPP is a picture story that helps to plan a visit to the Children's Museum of New Hampshire. MAPPS can be very helpful for children on the autism spectrum and their families.

You can make your own MAPP or follow one that has already been made.

MAPPS allows you to pick and choose what you would like to see. With MAPPS, you can also share with others by saving your visit to the museum as a picture story
